Something about me

Charlton Kenneth Jeffrey Howard (born 17 August 2003), professionally known as the Kid Laroi (stylised The Kid LAROI.), is an Australian rapper, singer and songwriter. Born in Waterloo, New South Wales, he originally gained recognition from his association and friendship with Juice Wrld while he was on tour in Australia.

He gained a local following before joining a partnership agreement with Lil Bibby's Grade A Productions and Columbia Records.
